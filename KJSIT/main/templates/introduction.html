An Intelligent Health Prediction and Emergency Assistance System

In the realm of healthcare, the integration of advanced technologies has paved the way for groundbreaking innovations. Our project, Med Assist, aims to revolutionize the way we approach disease diagnosis and emergency response. Leveraging the power of machine learning alogorithms, this project focuses on predicting both high-end and low-end diseases based on presented symptoms. Additionally, it incorporates a crucial feature to swiftly identify and connect individuals with the closest hospital during emergencies.
